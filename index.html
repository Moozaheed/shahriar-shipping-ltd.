Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner in Customs Clearing & Logistics

Shahriar Shipping Ltd is a trusted and experienced Customs Clearing and Forwarding Agent in Bangladesh, providing reliable logistics and customs clearance solutions for businesses across multiple industries.

With professional expertise and strong operational capabilities, we work at all Customs Offices of Bangladesh, with special focus on Chattogram, Mongla, Dhaka, Benapole, and Pangaon. Our goal is to ensure smooth, efficient, and compliant customs handling for all types of goods.

Our Process

How It Works

A streamlined, transparent process that ensures your shipments are handled efficiently from start to finish.

01

Document Submission

You provide us with your shipment details and required documents. We review and prepare everything needed for customs clearance.

02

Verification & Filing

Our experts verify all documentation, classify goods accurately, and file the customs declaration with the relevant authorities.

03

Customs Coordination

We liaise directly with customs officers, port authorities, and relevant agencies to expedite assessment and approval.

04

Cargo Release & Delivery

Once cleared, your cargo is released and we coordinate final transport to your warehouse or designated destination.
